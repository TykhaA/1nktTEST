ABOUT

Welcome to 1kNode, an innovative venture born from the entrepreneurial spirit and technological expertise that propelled Academy Smart(link) to the forefront of the IT industry. Established by the visionaries behind Academy Smart, a distinguished IT managed service provider since 2009, 1kNode emerges as a dynamic force in the blockchain domain, poised to redefine how businesses leverage blockchain technology.

Our Legacy: The Foundation of 1kNode

At the heart of 1kNode's inception are the founders of Academy Smart, a company celebrated for its exceptional track record in delivering cutting-edge IT solutions. With over a decade of experience, Academy Smart has cultivated a rich legacy of excellence, characterized by its extensive portfolio of over 200 successfully completed projects. The company's commitment to innovation and quality has earned the trust of global giants, including Microsoft, Sodexo, and ITSoft, marking it as a leader in the IT service industry. Operating with a talented team of over 100 employees spread across five offices worldwide, Academy Smart's global footprint and diverse expertise provide the solid foundation upon which 1kNode is built.

At 1kNode, we are more than just a service provider; we are your strategic partner in the blockchain revolution. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction is unwavering.
